Prasanthi Vidhya Bhavan was started in Teacher's colony Erode in 1985 with 50 students under visionary of our beloved correspondent late Thiru A.RajaManikam sir impressed by the quality of Education. Prasanthi spreaded its wings in 1988 in 8 acres as Prasanthi Vidhya Bhavan Matric Higher Secondary school. Prasanthi institutions operates under T.S.Educational Trust under the secretaryship of late Thiru A.RajaManikam sir consequently in early 90's. The schools were functioning with strength of around 800 students who were coming in and around Erode. Now the school is renovating with special infra structure for 20th century. The pathway payed by founder thiru A.RajaManikam sir are being followed as stepping stones for our success.
