ACADEMICS

The school provides quality education for the students from LKG to XII standard. The school follows state board syllabus.

General education is followed from KG to X standard.

In Higher Secondary, we offer the following groups:

Group 1: English, Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Biology

Group 2: English, Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Computer Science

Group 3: English, Commerece, Accountacy, Economics, Business Mathematics

  • Language: Medium of insturctions only in English. Students can opt Tamil as second language
  • For the students opting Group 1 (Biology) NEET classes are conducted and the students getting into the coaching is optional.
  • For the students opting Group 3, CPT classes are conducted and the students getting into the coaching is optional.
  • Extra coaching classes do not hinder their regular academic schedule.
  • For the students in Commerce stream, Cambridge ESOL exams are conducted after training. These exams are conducted by the British Council and they are provided with Cambridge certificate.

CAREER GUIDANCE:

Students in classes 9th and 11th are guided to select their career by means of Career Guidance Classes held periodically. By means of this class, they know their skills and talents and choose the course they can flourish in.
